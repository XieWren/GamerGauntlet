Story of the Gamer's Gauntlet

In June 2021, I was tasked with designing a website as part of my Web Development module. This webpage was what came out of my brain.


Anyways, allow me to justify my decision to you people I don't even know exist.


So what is the Gamer's Gauntlet?

It's not even that bad of an idea to begin with. I had only recently downloaded Steam and got into PC gaming. But when I saw the global achievement stats, I felt something was off. In games with more than 1 ending, the more difficult ending always had a lesser completion percentage.


This was a bit weird for me. Why do people play a game, I asked myself, if they weren't going to complete it? Did they just not see the fun in it?


I thought back on the silly challenges I previously saw content creators on YouTube attempt. Obviously, many of them were too dumb for the devs to have coded into the game. Use a bingo board? Grass% speedruns? Way too silly.


But. It was fun.


So here we are.


A place for me to spread/collate my wacky ideas for you people, who want to play a game, but in a new, different way.


Welcome to the Gamer's Gauntlet.
