How difficulties are chosen:

There are 5 possible difficulty options, being:

Easy

This challenge can be done so long as you have completed the game once/well enough to know most of the mechanics involved, and some time.

Could be Worse

Difficulty on par with easy, but may take a bit more work to do. Such challenges are like the low% speedrun to any% speedruns - they are virtually the same, but you need more work to complete them.

Starting to Suffer

While challenges on this difficulty may be rough, these are the type of challenges that streamers always play around with, to 'entertain' the audience. (Viewers are a sadistic bunch of people if you let them.) You might suffer along the way, but when you finally complete the challenge, you realise it's nothing compared to the final level of difficulty.

Massive Luck Required

Challenges in this category, depend on not just a player's skill in order to achieve, but more often than not also requires copious amounts of RNG in order to get anything done. So basically, even if you have suffered enough, if you don't have your lucky everything with you, you will continue to suffer. No worrise though, you get the chance to experience/learn the 5 stages of grief. (And who wouldn't want that?)

Help.

So, here's the thing. There won't be that many challenges that fall in this category, simply because when challenges become too hard, people aren't gonna play them anymore. But, if you are a masochist and have several hundred hours in a game, completed it up to 112%, and collected all 700 pinecones, you could give them a try. Enjoy eternal suffering!
