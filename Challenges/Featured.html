1st: Gun% (Hollow Knight)

Enter the Pantheon, inspired by Enter the Gungeon and Hollow Point, by u/Sir-Wow on Reddit


I will admit I am biased towards the memes of all the Hollow Knight characters holding guns, way before the mod even came out. But honestly, after spending tens of hours trying to defeat the stupid Pantheon of Hallownest, it's very satisfying to see all the bosses die before they can do a thing to you. There is a lesson in that, I think.

Coupled with the Gun's ability to propel the Knight into the air, – one of those things so ridiculous it becomes funny – I really have no choice but to give this 1st place, despite my complaints about it making the game too easy.

When you spend 45 minutes per try of the Pantheon of Hallownest, then die to Pure Vessel right before Abs Rad, you learn to enjoy the simpler things in life. Like hurling a gun around like an American, except Americans can't fly with their guns.

2nd: No Movement Keys (Terraria)

Video Thumbnail for the no WASD Challenge, by ShyGuyMask


While 1st Place was done because it was funny, the video on playing Terraria without Movement Keys was what got me really interested in the idea of weird challenges in games.

Terraria has always been a game that I found trouble in (found the mobile user), so it was really interesting to see people pulling off challenges like these. It made me think more about all the games I played, and how it would be great to compile these ideas for me and everyone to enjoy.

Go watch ShyGuyMask's videos. They are all have the same style, and are great in their own way.

3rd: Manhunt as a Hunter (Minecraft)

Page on an old comic featuring GeorgeNotFound and Dream, by @shyrilship on Twitter


I gotta be honest, I was really worried writing this, but I think it came out alright. Quite proud of this one, honestly. There was a lot of really in-depth ideas I got to write about, and I am glad I managed to put in some of my own ideas.

Never found much interest in Minceraft, but there is something appealing in playing with friends.

Also, no hate, but the Hunters seem to like rushing into situations without thinking. The number of situations where I found myself writing about choosing to conduct an ambush, and for the Speedrunner to use Explosives.
